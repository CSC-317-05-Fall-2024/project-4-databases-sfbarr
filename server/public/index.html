Kyoto is an amazing city in Japan that’s full of history and culture. It has beautiful temples like Kinkaku-ji and Kiyomizu-dera that are super interesting to visit. You can walk around Gion and maybe even see a geisha! The bamboo grove in Arashiyama is really peaceful, and in spring, the cherry blossoms make everything look gorgeous. With delicious food and so much to see, Kyoto is definitely a place you don’t want to miss!"
